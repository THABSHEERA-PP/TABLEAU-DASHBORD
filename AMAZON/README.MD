The Amazon Sales Analysis dashboard created using Tableau provides a clear overview of sales performance for April 2023. It shows key metrics such as total number of products, total quantity sold, total profit, total revenue, and overall sales, helping to understand the business performance at a glance. The dashboard also compares sales and profit across different product categories like beauty, electronics, fashion, and books, showing which categories perform best. It includes insights into customer behavior, such as preferred payment methods like credit card, UPI, and cash on delivery. Additionally, it displays discount percentages by category and the quantity of products sold in each category. The dashboard also highlights customer regions with the highest ratings, such as North America and Asia. Overall, this dashboard helps in analyzing sales trends, customer preferences, and product performance, making it useful for better business decision-making.
