A heart disease risk prediction dashboard in Tableau is an interactive data visualization tool that helps analyze and predict a person’s risk of developing heart disease using various health indicators. In this dashboard, important symptoms and risk factors such as chest pain, shortness of breath, fatigue, palpitation, dizziness, swelling, and cold sweats are visually represented along with medical conditions like hypertension, high cholesterol, diabetes, smoking habits, and obesity. Tableau combines these inputs into charts, graphs, and filters, allowing users to easily explore patterns and relationships between different factors. By using calculated fields and predictive analytics, the dashboard can highlight high-risk individuals and provide insights for early diagnosis and prevention. This makes it a powerful tool for healthcare professionals to make informed decisions and for individuals to better understand their health condition.
